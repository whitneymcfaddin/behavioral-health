Suicide

7 things to help manage your suicidial ideations right now

  • Recognition
    • Recognize that depression and hopelessness can distort your perceptions and reduce your ability to make good decisions.
  • What is the cause?
    • Suicidal feelings are the result of treatable problems such as hopelessness. Immediately tell someone (trusted or a professional) how you feel. Talking about it can help to locate the cause of these thoughts and treat it right away.
  • Fake it 'till you make it
    • Act as if there are other options instead of suicide, even if you may not see them right now.
  • Stay busy
    • You must make plans. Make plans with friends or to do things alone. Being busy can help you fill your brain with things other than sucidal ideations.
  • Lists
    • It's cheesy but it works. Create a list of pleasant events. List absolutely anything and everything you find plesant.
  • Defusion
    • Redirect your focus to some meaningful activity. Physically move into a different room, listen to music, go for a walk, read a book, etc. Become absorbed in something new, pay attention to something real in the present moment, this is mindfulness.
  • Question everything you think
    • What is the evidence for this thought? Against it?
    • Am I basing this thought on fact or feeling?
    • Is this thought black and white, when reality is more complicated?
    • Could I be misinterpreting the evidence? Am I making assumptions?
    • Could my though be an exaggeration of what's true?
    • Am I having this thought out of habit?
