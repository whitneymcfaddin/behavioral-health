Anxiety

7 tools to help manage your anxiety right now

  • Stand or sit up straight
    • Now take a deep breath in and when you release it say one (outloud or to yourself). Count to 30.
  • Move
    • Physically move around. Stetch, crunches, a walk or even a run. It can be as little as 5 minutes but just move around to direct energy somewhere other than your thoughts.
  • Distractions
    • Have a few go to healthy distractions that you enjoy. This sounds simple because it is. Just distract yourself for 2 minutes with something like a game, drawing, a song and if it doesn't work the first time, do it for 2 more minutes.
  • Talk yourself down
    • “Is this worry realistic? Is this really likely to happen? If the worst possible outcome happens, what would be so bad about that? Could I handle that? What might I do? If something bad happens, what might that mean about me? Is this really true or does it just seem that way? What might I do to prepare for whatever may happen?”
  • Mantra
    • You can do this anywhere anytime. Just stop what you're doing, sit or stand in a comfortable position and say over and over "be here now". Allow yourself to be and feel exactly what you are at that moment. Do this for 2-5 minutes.
  • Acceptance
    • Accept your anxiety as it is. And then call it as it is. Temporary, harmless, and can be controled.
  • 3-3-3
    • Look around you and name three things you see. Then, name three sounds you hear. Finally, move three parts of your body --- your ankle, fingers, or arm.
